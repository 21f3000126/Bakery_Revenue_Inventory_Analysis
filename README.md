# Bakery Revenue & Inventory Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales and inventory data from Golden Crust Bakery, Kanpur, to identify opportunities for revenue growth, profitability improvement, and inventory optimization.
The bakery lacked a structured system for sales tracking and inventory management. To address this challenge, sales and inventory data were manually collected over 30 days and analyzed using Microsoft Excel to generate actionable business insights and strategic recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Golden Crust Bakery faced several operational challenges:

- Lack of sales tracking and financial visibility
- Inefficient inventory management
- Stockouts and overstocking of products
- Revenue loss due to unstructured decision-making

The objective of this project was to leverage data-driven analysis to improve profitability, inventory control, and overall business performance.

---

## Dataset

### Data Collection

- 30 days of manually collected sales data
- 2,523 sales records
- Product-level inventory and pricing information
- Daily transaction tracking
- SKU-level performance data

Each record included:

- Product Name
- SKU
- Item Type
- Cost Price
- Selling Price
- Units Sold
- Opening Stock
- Closing Stock
- Date
- Day of Week

## Key Findings

### Revenue & Profit Analysis

- Rich Cream products generated the highest revenue (₹52,450).
- Fresh Cream products delivered the highest profit (₹16,100).
- Pastries showed strong performance in both revenue and profitability.
- Bread and Dry Cake categories contributed relatively low profits.

### ABC Inventory Analysis

Products were classified based on their contribution to total sales:

- **Class A:** High-priority products that should always remain in stock.
- **Class B:** Moderate-priority products requiring periodic review.
- **Class C:** Low-priority products suitable for reduced stocking or discontinuation.

### Weekly Sales Trends

- Saturday recorded the highest revenue (₹41,145).
- Sunday was the second-highest performing day (₹31,955).
- Friday showed increasing demand leading into the weekend.
- Tuesday generated the lowest sales due to the bakery's weekly closure.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### Pricing & Profitability

- Optimize pricing for low-margin products.
- Introduce premium product variants.
- Improve profitability of high-revenue, low-margin SKUs.

### Inventory Management

- Maintain adequate stock levels for Class A products.
- Minimize inventory holding of Class C products.
- Implement inventory planning using ABC classification.

### Marketing & Promotions

- Launch weekend promotional campaigns.
- Introduce loyalty programs for repeat customers.
- Bundle slow-moving products with best-selling items.

### Operations

- Increase staffing during weekends.
- Utilize Tuesday closures for inventory audits and staff training.

## Business Impact
